# Product

## 1. User type

- 1.1 Guest: No account and no purchase.
- 1.2 Free user: having account but no purchase.
- 1.3 Member: having account and at least 1 time of purchase.

## 2. User Portrait

- 2.1 Jobless people.
- 2.2 New grads lack of professional experiences.
- 2.3 People who have just changed careers.
- 2.4 Age between 20 to 40.

## 3. Core hypothesis

- 3.1 Users are willing to pay for resume imporvement.
- 3.2 Users are willing to pay for job seeking suggestions.
- 3.3 Users are willing to pay for cover letter generation.
- 3.4 The strongest motivation is uploading from users of their existing resume.

## 4. Target

- 4.1 Access restriction of several advanced functions will be applied to free users in this AI platform.
- 4.2 This AI platform will mainly focus on resume improvement.
- 4.3 This AI platform should try to increase the purchasing rate of users.
- 4.4 Operation and interface of this AI platform must be easy and convenient to users.
